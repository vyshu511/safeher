Beginner Yoga Sequence

Beginner Yoga Routine

  1. Gentle warm-up and breathing exercises
  2. Basic standing poses for foundation
  3. Simple seated and reclining poses
  4. Focus on alignment and breath awareness
  5. End with relaxation pose

Best Time to Practice

Morning or evening, on empty stomach

Benefits

  • Builds foundational strength
  • Improves body awareness
  • Introduces basic breathing techniques
  • Gentle on joints and muscles
  • Reduces stress and anxiety

Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation)

12-Step Sequence

  1. Pranamasana (Prayer pose)
  2. Hasta Uttanasana (Raised arms pose)
  3. Hasta Padasana (Hand to foot pose)
  4. Ashwa Sanchalanasana (Equestrian pose)
  5. Dandasana (Stick pose)
  6. Ashtanga Namaskara (Salute with eight parts)
  7. Bhujangasana (Cobra pose)
  8. Adho Mukha Svanasana (Downward facing dog)
  9. Ashwa Sanchalanasana (Equestrian pose)
  10. Hasta Padasana (Hand to foot pose)
  11. Hasta Uttanasana (Raised arms pose)
  12. Pranamasana (Prayer pose)

Best Time to Practice

At sunrise, facing the sun, on empty stomach

Benefits

  • Full-body workout in one sequence
  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Enhances flexibility and strength
  • Boosts metabolism and aids weight loss
  • Balances all body systems
