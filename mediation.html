Better Sleep

Fall asleep faster and wake up refreshed with our guided sleep meditations.

Tips for Better Sleep:

  • Establish a consistent sleep schedule
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • Keep your bedroom cool and dark
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bed
  • Limit caffeine after 2pm

Meditation Poses:

  • Corpse Pose (Savasana): Lie flat on your back with arms at sides
  • Legs-Up-the-Wall Pose (Viparita Karani): Lie on your back with legs vertically up a wall
  • Child's Pose (Balasana): Kneel and fold forward with arms extended

Reduced Stress

Proven techniques to lower cortisol levels and increase resilience.

Stress Reduction Tips:

  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Take regular breaks throughout the day
  • Engage in physical activity daily
  • Limit news and social media consumption
  • Practice gratitude journaling

Meditation Techniques:

  • Mindful Breathing: Focus solely on your breath for 5-10 minutes
  • Body Scan: Systematically relax each part of your body
  • Loving-Kindness Meditation: Send positive thoughts to yourself and others

Improved Focus

Sharpen your concentration and productivity with mindfulness exercises.

Focus Improvement Tips:

  • Work in 25-minute focused intervals (Pomodoro technique)
  • Minimize multitasking
  • Create a dedicated workspace
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day
  • Take short movement breaks every hour

Focus Meditation Techniques:

  • Single-Point Focus: Concentrate on a candle flame or small object
  • Mantra Meditation: Repeat a calming word or phrase
  • Walking Meditation: Focus on each step and breath
