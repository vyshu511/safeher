✨ Yoga Mudras for Healing & Balance ✨

Gyan Mudra

Benefits: Enhances focus, reduces stress, boosts memory.

Duration: 30-45 mins daily.

Best Time: Morning meditation.

Prithvi Mudra

Benefits: Strengthens bones, improves digestion.

Duration: 15-30 mins daily.

Best Time: Morning (empty stomach).

Varun Mudra

Benefits: Hydrates skin, balances fluids.

Duration: 15 mins, 2x/day.

Best Time: Anytime (avoid excess).

Prana Mudra

Benefits: Boosts immunity, energizes body.

Duration: 20-30 mins daily.

Best Time: When fatigued.

Apana Mudra

Benefits: Relieves constipation, detoxifies.

Duration: 15-20 mins, 2x/day.

Best Time: After meals.

Shunya Mudra

Benefits: Relieves ear pain, enhances intuition.

Duration: 10-15 mins/session.

Best Time: When needed.

Surya Mudra

Benefits: Aids weight loss, boosts metabolism.

Duration: 5-15 mins daily.

Best Time: Morning.

Vayu Mudra

Benefits: Reduces gas, calms nervous system.

Duration: 10-15 mins.

Best Time: After meals.
