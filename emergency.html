Safety Tips

Important safety information for women traveling alone

Ride Safety

Always verify the vehicle and driver details before entering. Sit in the back seat, preferably behind the driver. Share your ride details with someone you trust.

Walking Safety

Stay in well-lit areas and avoid shortcuts through alleys or deserted areas. Walk confidently and be aware of your surroundings. Keep your phone accessible but not visibly out.

Hotel Safety

When traveling alone, request a room near the elevator. Don't accept help with your luggage from strangers. Use all locking devices on your door and windows.

Tech Safety

Keep your phone charged and carry a portable charger. Save emergency contacts with quick-dial shortcuts. Use location-sharing apps when meeting new people.

Taxi Safety

Use licensed taxis or reputable ride-sharing services. Check that the taxi has functioning seat belts. Note the taxi number and share it with someone.

Social Safety

Never leave your drink unattended. Have a plan for getting home safely. Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, remove yourself from the situation.
